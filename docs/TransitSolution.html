Engage with riders and monitor safety

Configurable tools to inform the public of service changes, manage transit safety reports and mitigation plans, monitor the performance of transit agencies, and partner with volunteers to keep transit stops safe and clean.

The ArcGIS Solutions for Local Government's Transit Solution includes a series of focused maps and applications designed to help transit agencies leverage their geographic information and the ArcGIS Platform to improve workflows and enhance services delivered to riders. Configurable apps and templates are available for rider outreach, adopt-a-stop program management, and transit safety management.
