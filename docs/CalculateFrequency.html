Calculate Transit Service Frequency

Analyze the frequency of transit service

Calculate the frequency of scheduled public transit service available within one or more specified time windows at public transit stops, along public transit lines, at points of interest, or in areas.

The powerful Calculate Transit Service Frequency tool can calculate public transit frequency statistics:

  • At public transit stops
  • Along public transit line segments between pairs of connected stops
  • At specified points of interest using the stops within a specified travel time or distance
  • For all areas within a specified travel time or distance of public transit stops

Statistics calculated include the number of transit runs and runs per hour during the time window and the minimum, maximum, and average headway. You can choose to separate the counts by transit line or combine all lines together, and you can calculate statistics for one or more time windows.
